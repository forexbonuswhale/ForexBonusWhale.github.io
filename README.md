## Usage

### Navigation
1. Click logo or "Home" to go to homepage
2. Use navigation menu to switch between sections
3. Use search bar to find specific brokers
4. Click any broker card to view full details

### Sorting & Filtering
1. Select sort option (Most Popular, Highest Rated, Newest, Oldest)
2. Choose filter (All, Active, Inactive)
3. Results update automatically

### Leaving Reviews
1. Click on any broker to view details
2. Scroll down to "Leave Your Review" section
3. Enter your name, rating, and review
4. Click "Submit Review"
5. Your review will appear immediately
